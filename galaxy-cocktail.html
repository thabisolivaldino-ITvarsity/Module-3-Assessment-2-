GALAXY COCKTAIL

Prep Time:10min

Yield:4 min

Ingrediants







steps

  1. In a blender, combine tequila, grenadine, blue curaçao, lemonade, and ice. blend thoroughly until smooth.
  2. In a separate blender, combine vodka, blue curaçao, peach schnapps, lemonade, and ice. Blend thoroughly until smooth.
  3. Rim the edge of a hurricane glass with pink sugar and pour in the blended mixtures, alternating colors.
  4. Garnish with a lemon slice.
