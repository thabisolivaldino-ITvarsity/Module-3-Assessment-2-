Ingrediants







steps

  1. Swirl dulce de leche around the (inner-side of) serving glasses and freeze it while you are preparing the milkshakes.
  2. In a blender, blend ice cream and milk till its smooth.
  3. Pour this into the glasses and freeze till you prepare whipped cream.
  4. In a bowl, beat heavy cream, sugar and dulce de leche until stiff peaks are formed.
  5. Top milkshake with whipped cream and garnish it with churros, chocolate bars and sprinkle some cinnamon powder.
