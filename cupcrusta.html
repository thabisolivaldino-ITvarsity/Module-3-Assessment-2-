CROUSTILLANTES

Prep Time:10min

Yield:7min

Ingrediants






steps

  1. In a container, mix the peanut butter, maple syrup and almond powder until you get a paste.
  2. Separate the dough into 14 and form balls. Divide the balls between 14 mini muffin pans. With the help of the back of a glass squeeze them.
  3. Melt the chocolate in a water bath. Once melted, add the coconut oil and mix until the whole is homogeneous. Then the corn flakes. Make sure that each flake is coated with chocolate.
  4. Place 1 tsp of chocolate corn flakes on the previous preparation. Tamp lightly with your fingers. Reserve in a cool place until the chocolate is completely frozen.
